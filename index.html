Some of My Favorite Books:

I love reading. This is an unranked, non-exhaustive list of some of my favorite books:
  • What Is Life? The Physical Aspect of the Living Cell, Erwin Schrodinger
    • Written in 1943, in What is Life? Schrodinger argued that based on physical first principles genetic inheritance must take the form of an "aperiodic crystal". DNA was later discovered in the 1950s.
  • The Contrarian: Peter Thiel and Silicon Valley's Pursuit of Power
    • A biography of Peter Thiel, the controversial and visionary venture capitalist. Thiel is also the founder of Confinity, PayPal and Palentir Technologies.
  • Tort Law in America: An Intellectual History, G. Edward White
    • A history of tort law in the United States. Surprisingly, the history of tort law is a fascinating subject.
  • Dancing Naked in the Mind Field, Kary Mullis
    • A autobiography of Kary Mullis, winner of the Nobel Prize in chemistry for the discovery of PCR.
