Some of My Favorite Books:

I love reading. This is an unranked, non-exhaustive list of some of my favorite books:
  • What Is Life? The Physical Aspect of the Living Cell, Erwin Schrodinger
    • Written in 1943, in What is Life? Schrodinger argued that genetic inheritance must take the form of an "aperiodic crystal".
  • The Contrarian: Peter Thiel and Silicon Valley's Pursuit of Power
    • A biography of Peter Thiel, the controversial and visionary venture capitalist. Thiel is also the founder of Confinity, PayPal and Palentir Technologies. Competition is for Losers
  • Dancing Naked in the Mind Field, Kary Mullis
    • An autobiography of Kary Mullis, winner of the 1993 Nobel Prize in chemistry for the discovery of PCR.
  • Differential Forms in Algebraic Topology, Raoul Bott and Loring Tu
    • An excellent introduction to differntial forms in algebraic topology.
  • Runaway Horses, Yukio Mishima
    • An meditation on the blessings and burdens of patriotism, responsibility and adulthood.
  • American Lion, Jon Meachm
    • Biography of U.S. President Andrew Jackson.
